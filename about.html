A little bit about our story

Welcome to Grads Mobile App! A leading provider of student-specific resource solutions for college students. Our company was founded in 2020 with a vision to help students all across. Since then, we have been dedicated to providing our students with the highest quality student-specific resources and exceptional customer service.

At Grads, we believe in simplifying and enhancing the lives of students. Our goal is to provide practical and efficient solutions that optimize their academic experiences. We understand the unique needs of students and strive to meet them through our innovative app.

In addition to our commitment to students, we are also passionate about giving back to the community. As part of our corporate social responsibility, we support various charitable causes and organizations, including sponsoring students and championing environmental sustainability to reduce our carbon footprint.

Thank you for considering Grads for your academic and student needs. We are confident that our solutions will exceed your expectations, and we look forward to serving you. If you have any questions or would like to learn more about our company, please don't hesitate to contact us.

Meet our team members

Nalindrani Malimage: Founder and CEO - B.A. (Hons) Econ, CIMA U-K, M.S (U.S.A)

" I started Grads as my passion project. This is a free app and it's built to help students. I was motivated to build this app based on my hardships in the United States. As a student, it's very difficult to find resources on campus and takes time to get used to the campus. Grads can help students find resources easily while staying secure. I built this app based on an incident in which my friend was sexually assaulted while walking to the campus. It's very important to help students find reliable resources."
