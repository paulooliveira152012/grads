What is Grads?

Grads is a FREE and secure marketplace mobile APP for all students. Students can not only find the products and services they need but also profit from the platform by listing items for sale, apartments or couches for rent, or any other services they can provide.

Download our free app today to see how we stand out and the unique features that make us special! Available on IOS or Android.

As a safety measure, an ".edu" email address is required to sign up.
