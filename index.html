Grads is a FREE marketplace for all students. It's secure and helps students earn extra cash by selling products/services. Also, helps students save cash by buying things at a much lower cost. Following are a few features you might find useful:

Check out what makes us unique!

  • Free app for all students.
  • Verified users to prevent scammers.
  • List tutoring services and earn money while helping other students.
  • Buy used textbooks from senior students to save money.
  • Anonymously rate roommates for stress-free living arrangements.
  • Offer rides to other students and earn money through "Rideshare".
  • List your room for short-term stays during holidays under "Housing".
  • Get rid of extra food or unused items by listing them under "Food" or "Miscellaneous".
  • Multiple payment options available including "Stripe".
  • And many more exciting features!
